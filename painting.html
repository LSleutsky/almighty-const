Painting

Painting a new room or painting your house can truly make a world of difference. With the right type of quality paint, and with the right highly trained patterns, truly amazing designs and patterns can be accomplished, and whether you decide to paint your bedroom, kitchen, or bathroom, we can guarantee that you will have a feeling of calm pass over you whenever you enter your freshly painted room...

Sheetrock

Sheetrock (or drywall as you probably know it) is the very basis of your home. When the shell of the house is built, the walls are sheetrocked to give you a normal looking living space. There is not much more to be said about sheetrock, besides that we install it - we do new sheetrock - we fix sheetrock - we replace sheetrock - and so on and so forth. Sometimes, you will find that some walls have been wallpapered. Yes, it's true - though wallpaper isn't the most common thing to do nowadays, some houses will be found with wallpaper, and we can expertly remove the wallpaper to cause the least amount of damage to the underlying wall, and go to work on leveling out the sheetrock that lies beneath, and making it look like a normal wall...

Tape & Spackle

Sometimes when there are all little nail and / or screw holes in your walls from hanged paintings, or if the kids got a little too rambunctious and decided to treat your walls like their personal punching bags, sheetrock can take quite a beating. Instead of completely removing and replacing entire sheets of sheetrock, however, we are very good at repairing it, via the tape and spackle method. Large holes, small holes, cuts, dings, whatever the issue is, we can fix your walls to look good as new. Sometimes we use traditional spackle, and sometimes we use quick drying spackle. One thing we never do, is use shortcuts...
