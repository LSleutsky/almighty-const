Landscaping

Landscaping is more than just mowing your lawn and planting some bushes here and there. Landscaping is an art form. From new homes, to well-established homes, landscaping can greatly beautify your home's curb appeal. Proper trees, plants, shrubs, etc. can be a nice focal point, and really give your home's exterior those finishing touches, in just the right amounts. With a custom designed landscaped that is designed for you specifically, you not only increase the value of your home, you increase the value of the time you spend enjoying the exterior of your house...

Hardscaping

Hardscaping is a somewhat new term to describe the use of various stone in your landscape. At Almighty Construction, we use all types of different materials, from huge boulders to make for an eye-popping landscape, to smaller decorative stone that may emphasize plant life. We can create custom patios using various pavers, bluestone, and many other natural stones that enhance the look and feel of the outside. And with stone walkways, retaining walls, and ponds, your landscape could very well be the talk of the town...

Fencing

From custom wood fencing, to chain link fences and PVC fences, the options are limitless! Whether you are looking to protect & enclose your property, or just add curb appeal, different types of fences can really add value and cosmetic improvements to your home. A fenced in property provides all kind of value and benefits to homeowners. Besides the obvious safety and privacy, some homeowner's insurance companies may actually provide a discounted rate just for having a fenced yard...

Design & Build

You provide the ideas and the inspiration, and we'll provide the end result. Anything you may have thought of to enhance the look and value of your home, we can make into a reality, right before your very eyes!
