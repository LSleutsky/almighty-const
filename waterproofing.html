Waterproofing

Whether you have a basement or a crawlspace, each and every home has what is called a water table underneath it. Whenever there is a rain fall (or snow thaw), water seeps in and saturates into the topsoil, then absorbs underground, causing the water table to first rise and then seep into your basement, seeking the path of least resistance. Whether your floor leaks or your walls leak, Almighty Construction will evaluate and design a pressure relief water management system. You cannot stop the water from trying to intrude into your home, but with our expertise, you can certainly manage it, and keep it from ever entering...

Mold Remediation

To be blunt - mold & mildew is NASTY! Mold is a species which is still being very heavily studied today, because not much is known about these fungi. There are many different types of mold - some are visible, some are invisible - and mold thrives in dark, damp environments. Within 48 hours, mold can begin to grow and breed, and it multiplies and spreads exponentially. Due to the uncertain and unpredictable nature of mold, it is unwise to attempt self-cleaning; it is best to have certified professionals diagnose the issue, and come up with a containment & remediation plan, which is where Almighty Construction will be your leg-up...

Exterior Drainage | Structural Repair

While pressure relief management systems are vital in keeping water from entering your home due to rising water table levels, sometimes water can find its way in due to the grade of your property. With an exterior drainage system, not only will water be diverted from entering through the windows, over top of the foundation, and through exterior cracks, but it will also help tremendously with your yard filling up with water. Exterior drainage schematics are precise, and for your home only.
